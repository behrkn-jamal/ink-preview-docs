InkPreview

Design System Specification v1.0

1. Visual Philosophy: "Ink & Obsidian"

The "Ink & Obsidian" philosophy positions InkPreview as a premium, luxury tool. It emphasizes high contrast, depth, and a "dark-room" studio atmosphere.

  • Art-Centric: UI recedes to let tattoo artwork be the focus.
  • Depth & Dimension: Glassmorphism and subtle shadows create a tactile experience.
  • Luxury Minimalism: Clean lines and high-end typography.

5. Component Library

  • Primary Button: Liquid Gold Gradient, Obsidian Black text, 12px radius.

6. Layout & Grid

Based on an 8px baseline grid.

  • Side Margins: 24px
  • Component Gap: 16px
  • Responsive: Margins reduce to 16px on compact screens.
